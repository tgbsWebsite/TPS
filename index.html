WELCOME TO THAKUR PUBLIC SCHOOL

Welcome to Thakur Public School, where education is a journey of discovery, integrity, and achievement. We are committed to nurturing confident learners, responsible citizens, and compassionate leaders prepared to thrive in an ever-evolving world.

Rooted in strong values and driven by academic excellence, our school provides an enriching environment where every child is encouraged to explore, question, create, and grow.

Vision & Mission

Vision

  • To promote all young learners towards becoming responsible global citizens who are intellectual and ethical in their approach to their own lives and the world.

Mission

  • To provide holistic education that nurtures scientific temper and ethical values, grooming the students to be worthy and responsible citizens.

Academics

A broad, balanced, and future-focused ICSE education designed to nurture curiosity and academic excellence.

Academic Excellence

At Thakur Public School, we follow the ICSE curriculum, ensuring that students receive a broad and balanced education designed to prepare them for future challenges. Our focus is on nurturing curiosity, conceptual understanding, and academic excellence.

Our Campus

Facilities

Thakur Public School offers a safe, inspiring and well-equipped campus designed to foster academic and personal growth.

Air-Conditioned Classrooms

Comfortable learning spaces that enhance focus and engagement.

Library and AV Rooms

Resource rich environments that encourage research, reading and interactive learning.

Science and Computer Facilities

Supporting practical exploration and digital literacy.

Sports Facilities

Swimming, badminton, basketball and table tennis to promote physical fitness and teamwork.

Safe Transport

Reliable school bus facilities in Kandivali East ensuring secure and convenient travel.

Safety and Security

Monitored campus with trained personnel ensuring student wellbeing at all times.

Sports and Physical Activities

Opportunities in games such as badminton, basketball, football and cricket that build confidence, teamwork and resilience.

Performing Arts

Music, dance and drama activities that allow children to express themselves and develop creative skills.

Events and Competitions

Students participate in assemblies, recitation, elocution, art and cultural events, giving them chances to perform, collaborate and shine.

ICSE Board Excellence

Admission Process

store

Admission Process - Offline

1

Obtaining the Brochure and application form for Admission, from the School office.

2

Submitting the duly completed application form and supporting documents to TPS Administrative office and paying the Registration fee within the specified period.

3

Informing about the admission decision by the school to the parents.

4

Acceptance of the admission by the parents and payment of applicable fees/charges/expenses.

5

Confirmation of admission by Thakur Public School.

computer

Admission Process - Online

1

Click on Admission enquiry on School website.

2

Add enquiry for admission

3

Purchase Admission Form by paying applicable charges

4

Submit the duly filled form along with the required documents

5

Pay registration charges

6

On verification of admission form and uploaded documents, get confirmation from the School on admission decision.

7

Pay the applicable fees/charges/expenses.

8

Confirmation of admission by Thakur Public School.
